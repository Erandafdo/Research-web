Elephant Pulse

An AI-driven platform to improve elephant welfare, monitor health, detect stress, and manage visitor insights — built for Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage.

🤖 Machine Learning 👁️ Computer Vision 🥦 Nutrition AI 📊 Visitor Analytics
Explore Research

Research Questions

The guiding scientific inquiries driving every module of Elephant Pulse.

🏆 Primary Research Question
"How can an integrated, AI-driven management system effectively improve both the physiological well-being of captive elephants and the operational efficiency of wildlife sanctuaries like the Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage?"
🩺
Sub-RQ 1 — Health Prediction

AI Health Prediction & Diagnosis

"How effectively can machine learning models (such as Random Forest and Gradient Boosting) predict an elephant's overall health status and specific clinical diagnoses using non-invasive vital signs and historical health records?"

👁️
Sub-RQ 2 — Stress Detection

Computer Vision & Stress Detection

"To what extent can localized computer vision algorithms, specifically utilizing Optical Flow on standard CCTV feeds, accurately detect and quantify stress-related physical behaviors in elephants without relying on cloud computing?"

🥦
Sub-RQ 3 — Nutrition

Nutritional Optimization (Food Chain)

"How can an AI forecasting engine optimize personalized feeding plans and predict future nutritional resource requirements for an elephant herd to ensure optimal health and minimize waste?"

📊
Sub-RQ 4 — Visitor Insight

Visitor Insight & Sanctuary Management

"What is the impact of integrating real-time visitor analytics, crowd density tracking, and dynamic tariff management on the economic sustainability and daily operational efficiency of a wildlife sanctuary?"

Key Features

Four intelligent subsystems working together to protect elephants and empower sanctuary operations.

Health Profiles

Monitor vitals, log daily health reports, and view AI-generated predictions per elephant.

Stress Detector

Fully local CV pipeline using Optical Flow on live CCTV or uploaded video — no cloud needed.

Food Chain AI

Personalized feeding plans with 7-day AI forecasts and full feeding history tracking.

Visitor Insights

Real-time visitor counts, revenue analytics, tariff management and crowd density forecasting.

System Modules

Two integrated subsystems covering elephant care and visitor management.

🐘 Elephant Care System

  • AI-generated elephant portraits per profile
  • Random Forest health status prediction (5 classes)
  • Gradient Boosting clinical diagnosis
  • Local Optical Flow stress detection via CCTV / MP4
  • Nutritional calculation engine with 7-day forecast

🎟️ Visitor Insight System

  • Analytics dashboard: revenue, live visitors, AI accuracy
  • Tariff engine with full CRUD for ticket pricing tiers
  • Manual check-in updates live occupancy in real time
  • Microservice architecture: FastAPI + MongoDB + Docker
  • Next.js admin & visitor-facing apps (containerised)

AI Models

Every prediction in Elephant Pulse is powered by a trained, explainable ML model running locally.

Model Algorithm Purpose Output
Health Classifier Random Forest Vitals analysis Healthy, Infection, Dehydration, etc.
Diagnosis Classifier Gradient Boosting Clinical insight Bacterial Infection, Arthritis, etc.
Stress Detector R(2+1)D Motion analysis (local) Stress Probability % / Normal Behavior
Nutrition Engine Rule-Based AI Feeding optimisation Morning/Evening KG split + Remarks
Visitor Care Function Random Forest Visitor analytics and care Visitor insights
